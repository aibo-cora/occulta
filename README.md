**Occulta**
In a world where your contacts and trust are locked inside messaging apps, Occulta gives you true control.

Collect verified public keys from friends, family, and colleagues through secure, in-person exchanges using Nearby Interaction — no servers, no phone numbers, no intermediaries.

Once exchanged, encrypt any file, photo, video, or document for anyone in your collection — and share it however you want: AirDrop, email, Messages, chat apps. Your data stays hidden from being analyzed and sold.

Key Features

• Secure, proximity-based key exchange via Nearby Interaction (iPhone 11+)
• Encrypt files and messages.
• Diceware verification words for easy, human-friendly confirmation
• Protected by Apple Secure Enclave — your private keys never leave your device
• No accounts, no servers, no tracking — pure offline-first privacy

Occulta is the cryptographic address book the world has been missing.
